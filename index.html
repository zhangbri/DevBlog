Production Sync-up Meeting

Production sync-up meetings happen every week on Saturday from 5:30 - 6:00 pm. The lead producer is in charge of facilitating this meeting and providing any important announcements. This is a quick meeting over a discord voice channel where we go over updates of each of the producers’ squad member progress of the tasks that were created in Jira. This is also a great source of communication in case there are problems presented or questions that need to be answered. We also go over the agendas of what the Studio-wide meeting would be focusing on tomorrow.

Hours Breakdown

Start of Sprint 1

  • Production Sync-up Meeting (30 minutes)
  • Leads Meeting (1 hour)
  • Studio-wide Meeting (3 hours)
    • Studio Announcements – 11:00 AM ~ 11:10 AM (10 mins)
    • Project Announcements – 11:10 AM ~ 11:20 AM (10 mins)
    • Theme Pitches – 11:20 PM ~ 12:20 PM (60 mins)
    • Squad Meeting – 12:20 AM ~ 1:20 PM (60 mins)
    • Department Meeting – 1:20 PM ~ 2:00 PM (40 mins)
  • Task Breakdown Implementation Questions Documentation (1 hour)
  • Logging and Updating Tasks on Jira (1 hour)
  • Playing Gunstar Heroes (30 minutes)
  • Production Work Session (2 hours)
